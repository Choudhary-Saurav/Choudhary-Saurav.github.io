Single-cell Genomics

My research focuses on leveraging single-cell technologies to understand cellular heterogeneity and gene regulation mechanisms. I work with large-scale scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq datasets to map transcriptional and epigenomic landscapes.

Multi-omics Integration

I develop and apply computational approaches to integrate multiple layers of genomic data, including:

  • Transcriptomics (RNA-seq, single-cell RNA-seq)
  • Epigenomics (ATAC-seq, scATAC-seq)
  • Genetic variation (GWAS, fine-mapping)

Computational Tools

I actively contribute to and develop bioinformatics pipelines using:

  • R/Bioconductor: Seurat, ArchR, SCAVENGE
  • Python: Data processing and analysis workflows
  • HPC environments: Large-scale genomic data analysis
